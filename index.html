CAN Bus Project

CAN communication is an industry standard protocol used in the automotive industry. As a part of Formula Electric at Berkeley, I built printed circuit boards to incorporate sensors for measuring temperature, speed, and electric current. Furthermore, I wrote C++ scripts to enable communication and data logging among the sensors.
